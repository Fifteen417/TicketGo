Frequently asked questions

These are the most commonly asked questions about TicketGo and billing.

Contact Us

We are here to help! You can reach our support team via:

  • Email: support@ticketgo.com
  • Phone: +886 2 2345 6789

Can I change my plan later?

Of course. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account settings. The new plan will take effect immediately.

What is your cancellation policy?

Tickets are refundable up to 7 days before the event. After that, you can resell them on our marketplace.

Can other info be added to an invoice?

Yes, you can add your company details and VAT number in the billing section before downloading invoices.

How does billing work?

We charge your card immediately upon purchase. You will receive a receipt via email instantly.

How do I change my account email?

Go to Settings > Profile to update your email address. A verification link will be sent to your new email.
